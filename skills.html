Government Training Schemes

PMKVY (Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana)

India's flagship skill development initiative that aims to enable and mobilize a large number of Indian youth to take up industry-relevant skill training.

  • Free Training
  • Government Certificate
  • Monetary Reward on Assessment
  • Placement Assistance
Apply Now

DDU-GKY (Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana)

Rural skill development program focusing on poor rural youth aged 15-35 years with training, placement, and career progression support.

  • Free Residential Training
  • Monthly Stipend
  • 100% Job Guarantee
  • Post-Placement Support
Apply Now

NSDC (National Skill Development Corporation)

Industry-led training programs designed to scale up skill development initiatives across the country with focus on employability.

  • Industry Aligned Curriculum
  • Recognized Certification
  • Employer Connect
  • Career Advancement
Apply Now

Eligibility & Benefits

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 15-35 years for most programs
  • Education: 5th to 12th standard (varies by course)
  • Priority for rural youth, women, SC/ST, minorities
  • Annual family income below specified limits
  • No prior formal employment

Training Benefits

  • Completely FREE training programs
  • Monthly stipend during training
  • Free boarding and lodging (residential courses)
  • Training kit and study materials
  • Government recognized certification
  • Insurance coverage during training

Post-Training Support

  • 100% job placement assistance
  • Minimum salary guarantee
  • Post-placement tracking for 1 year
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Entrepreneurship development support
  • Soft skills and personality development

Training Process

1

Registration & Counseling

Free career counseling to select the right course based on your interests, aptitude, and local employment opportunities.

2

Documentation & Verification

Submit required documents for eligibility verification and enrollment into the selected skill development program.

3

Training & Assessment

Comprehensive training with experienced instructors, practical sessions, and regular assessments to ensure skill acquisition.

4

Certification & Placement

Government certification upon successful completion and immediate placement assistance with partner employers.

5

Post-Placement Support

Continuous support for career progression, job retention, and professional development for sustained employment.
